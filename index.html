How to Play

Click the circles to the beat! When the approach circle reaches the inner circle, click it.

Use your mouse to move the cursor and Z / X or Left Click / Right Click to tap.

Hit objects closer to the perfect timing for higher scores: 300, 100, 50, or Miss.

Build your combo for score multipliers. Don't let your HP drop to zero!

Press ESC to pause during gameplay.
